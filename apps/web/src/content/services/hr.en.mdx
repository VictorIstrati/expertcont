HR services and personnel management for companies in Moldova: outsource the HR administration function to a specialised team — billed per covered employee, no minimum contract, with all monthly processes included: contracts, payroll, CNAS/CNAM reporting, and Moldovan Labour Code compliance.

**Note**: we don't offer recruiting services (job ads, CV screening, interviews). We handle exclusively the administration of employment relationships for the company's existing employees.

For most small and medium LLCs, hiring an in-house HR specialist isn't economically justified — but neither is neglecting the HR function. Mistakes in employment contracts, CNAS/CNAM reporting, or leave management can lead to significant fines from the State Labour Inspectorate. The ExpertCont team takes on all these processes under a predictable monthly fee.

## What ExpertCont HR services include

HR support covers employment-relationship administration for your existing employees — billed per covered employee (400 MDL/month/employee):

- **Documentary onboarding** — preparing contracts and job descriptions for employees you've already hired
- **Employment contracts** — individual and collective, aligned with the Labour Code of the Republic of Moldova
- **Payroll** — net, gross, CNAS and CNAM contributions, income tax withheld at source
- **CNAS, CNAM and State Tax Service reporting** — all monthly, quarterly, and annual declarations

- **Internal regulations and policies** — internal rules, HR policies
- **Termination and labour-dispute support** — legal procedures, documentation, representation
- **Inspection support** for the State Labour Inspectorate and ITM controls
- **GDPR compliance** for employee data
