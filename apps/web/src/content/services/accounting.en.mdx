Complete accounting services for companies in Moldova: monthly bookkeeping, on-time tax reporting and proactive accounting consulting, aligned with the National Accounting Standards (NAS) and the Tax Code of the Republic of Moldova.

Whether your business is just starting out or already has market experience, every economic entity needs at least one person responsible for bookkeeping, transaction recording and result interpretation. The ExpertCont team becomes that person — without the cost of hiring an in-house accountant and without the pressure of reporting periods.

## What our accounting services include

Accounting support and consulting cover the full monthly and annual cycle:

- **Primary bookkeeping** — recording primary documents in line with legal norms and accounting principles
- **Payroll calculation and HR reporting** to CNAS, CNAM and the State Tax Service
- **Tax declarations** — VAT, income tax, property tax, local taxes
- **Financial statements** — balance sheet, profit and loss, cash flow, changes in equity
- **Analytical accounting** — clients, suppliers, fixed assets, depreciation calculations
- **Accounting registers** — cash book, bank register, bank deposits register
- **Tax inspection support** and assistance with state authority controls
- **Accounting consulting** throughout the engagement

## Who it's for

Our accounting services work for every legal form in the Republic of Moldova — LLC, JSC, sole trader, ICS and NGO — across diverse industries: trade, IT, manufacturing, professional services, HoReCa, construction, transport.

In practice we serve both start-ups at first tax registration and mature companies with consolidated activity and extended reporting requirements. We match the package to your document volume, turnover and industry — see [packages and the calculator](/en/pricing) for an estimate.
